Sum Of n Natural Numbers Calculator

Enter in a number and this program will add every natural number from 1 to that number, giving you the sum.

Equation: (n / 2) * (n + 1)
Note: Does not accept or work with zero, negative numbers, or decimals, as these are not considered natural numbers.
